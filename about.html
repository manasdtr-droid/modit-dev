About MODIT

Building India's most trusted construction commerce platform

Our Story

The Future of Construction Commerce

MODIT was founded with a simple mission: make construction materials procurement as easy as ordering food. We're building India's quick-commerce platform for the construction industry, connecting contractors, builders and homeowners with premium building materials delivered in hours, not days.

The current pilot is live across Noida pincodes, with Bihar next in the rollout plan. MODIT leverages technology to ensure real-time inventory tracking, dynamic pricing and lightning-fast delivery. We partner with India's most trusted brands including UltraTech, Asian Paints, Polycab, Kajaria and Bosch to guarantee 100% genuine products.
